## 📋 Cold-outreach playbook

1. **Source organizers** from Eventbrite NZ, Humanitix NZ, Facebook event pages, Instagram event posts. Build a list of 100.
2. **Personalize each email** with: their actual event name, scene, city, and ONE specific detail (sold-out date, festival appearance, etc.).
3. **Send in batches of 20/day** Tuesday-Thursday at 10am-12pm NZ time.
4. **Follow up exactly twice**: day 5, day 12. Then stop.
5. **Track replies** in a spreadsheet (or your CRM). Target: 15-25% reply rate.
6. **Offer your first 5 organizers "free first event up to 200 tickets"** as case studies you can quote in future outreach.

## 🚫 What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't send the same template to 100 people — personalize the first line every time.
- ❌ Don't include attachments in cold emails (kills deliverability).
- ❌ Don't write "Hope you're well" — get to the point in line 1.
- ❌ Don't include links to login/signup pages — just `allsale.events`.
- ❌ Don't send Mondays or Fridays.
